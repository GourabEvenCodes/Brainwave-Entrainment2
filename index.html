Adventure of a Lifetime (Coldplay) — Focus Mix

Hemi-Sync · Headphones Required
BPM 112 Harmonic Fit: A=432 (−8 Hz) Beats: L 14 Hz / R 28 Hz

Technical Summary

Objective
Focus via beta-range entrainment.
Method
Hemi-sync style per-ear beats using amplitude-modulated carriers (headphones required).
Beat Programme
Left 14 Hz; Right 28 Hz — both factors of 112 BPM; 28 = 2 × 14.
Harmonic Fit
Master retuned to A=432 (−8 Hz) to explore the 432 Hz Solfeggio context for calming/well-being. No constant sine bed is sustained; carriers are used only to impart beats.
Production
SpectraLayers 10 → Cubase; added pads/keys; EQ and sidechain; ShaperBox 3; iZotope RX.
Monitoring & Use
Moderate listening level on headphones to preserve distinct L/R beat geometry.
Experimentation Path
  • A=444 to place C5 approx. 528 Hz (Solfeggio test, not used in this render).
  • Dolby Atmos session with controlled binaural downmix to test spatial separability and translation of per-ear beats.

Gravity (John Mayer) — Relax Mix

Isochronic · No Headphones Required
BPM 124 Key: G (A=440) Carrier for pulses: 392 Hz (G4) Beat: 4 Hz (Low-Theta)

Technical Summary

Objective
Relaxation support via low-theta entrainment.
Method
Isochronic pulses (single-channel amplitude modulation) for a strong frequency-following response.
Beat Programme
4 Hz chosen as an integer factor of 124 BPM to preserve rhythmic consonance.
Harmonic Fit
Key centre G (A=440). Use 392 Hz (G4) strictly as a modulation carrier for the 4 Hz pulses. No constant sine bed.
Production
SpectraLayers 10 → Cubase; rainy ambience and white-noise bed; reverb-forward vocals; EQ trims in upper mids; gentle LPF with low-mid support; ShaperBox 3; iZotope RX.
Monitoring & Use
Comfortable level; enjoyable on both speakers and headphones.

How it Works — Process (Per Clip)

Adventure of a Lifetime — Hemi-Sync Process

  • Target and strategy: Focus via per-ear beta beats (14 Hz L, 28 Hz R).
  • Temporal fit: 14 and 28 are factors of 112 BPM; 28 = 2 × 14.
  • Harmonic fit: Retune to A=432 (−8 Hz) for the 432 Hz Solfeggio context; carriers only impart beats (no constant sine bed).
  • Signal build: Ear-specific amplitude-modulated carriers, level-balanced under stems.
  • Mix notes: SpectraLayers 10 → Cubase; pads/keys; EQ and sidechain to carve space; ShaperBox 3; iZotope RX.
  • Experimentation: A=444 to place C5 approx. 528 Hz; Atmos → binaural downmix for spatial separability.
  • Monitoring: Headphones required at moderate level.

Gravity — Isochronic Process

  • Target and strategy: Low-theta support using 4 Hz isochronic pulses.
  • Temporal fit: 4 Hz as integer factor of 124 BPM to maintain temporal consonance.
  • Harmonic fit: Key G; use 392 Hz (G4) as modulation carrier only (no constant sine bed).
  • Signal build: 392 Hz tone amplitude-modulated at 4 Hz and blended beneath stems.
  • Mix notes: SpectraLayers 10 → Cubase; rain and white noise beds; reverb-forward vocals; EQ trims in upper mids; gentle LPF/low-mid lift; ShaperBox 3; RX utilities.
  • Monitoring: Speakers or headphones at comfortable level.
